Psychology

I am currently a student attending the University of California - Riverside. I am a third year psychology major, and have been at Riverside for all three years. I plan to work somewhere within the psychology field once I am given the opportunity or have the time, but as of now, I have yet to find the job for me. Thus, for now, I am continuing my psychology studies at the university in order to enchance my knowledge and education, so that I will be well prepared for the future.

In order to best improve my knowledge of psychology, I have talked to a number of psychologists in different areas of the field. These experts include but are not limited to IO Psychologists, those in the HR department, those that work with the elderly and just within general social support therapy, and those that work in an educational setting, either as a counselor or a teacher/professor.

Psychology is my passion. I study it in my free time, and always find myself reading books and articles on the latest subjects in psychology. I am always seeking to further my knowledge across all areas of psychology, and am thrilled to venture farther into the world of understanding the behavior of the individuals within our society.
